Purpose

The purpose of the literary arts magazine (Fragments of Heaven) is to share the creative works of individuals with other creative people in order to spark ideas, and showcase beautiful works to the general public.

Mission

We aspire to promote creativity among individuals, help dissipate artist/creator/shyness, and boost confidence among individuals. We also want to help individual artists become more well-known, either in their local communities or online, and act a resource to help them perfect their craft.
